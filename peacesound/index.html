How it works

The NOISE bed is steady broadband sound that masks disruptive noise; TONE shifts it from deep/brown to balanced/pink. RAIN and SURF add slow, predictable natural texture with no sudden transients. DRONE is a quiet low tone used purely for comfort — no entrainment, binaural, or “miracle frequency” claims.

Sensory note

If you’re sensitive to sound, start with Sensory‑Simple (one steady sound) and add layers only if they help. Layered sound can become overstimulating.

Using it well

Set the volume so the sound is almost forgettable, especially overnight — low volume and amber light are easier on sleep. DIM darkens the panel and requests a screen wake‑lock so audio keeps playing through the night on phones.

Safety

This is not medical treatment. Stop if you notice distress, headache, ear pain, or agitation. In a mental‑health emergency, contact local emergency services or a crisis line.
