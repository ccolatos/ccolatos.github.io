Artist Statement

My work emerges from a desire to discover something new. I create interactive, multi-sensory works layered in sound and gesture, utilizing a diverse array of materials of archetypal and experimental form. Installations and compositions are living components of a larger ecology, drawing from a range of influences and inspired artistry across mediums, methods and traditions in both technical and non-technical settings. Works contemplate sensory constituents of perception, interplay, contrasts between natural and artificial, emotion, imagination, presence, permanence and ephemerality. Experiments and designs are intended to evoke presence and personal meaning, and shift with each participant’s interpretation in a particular moment. Emergent and conceptual frameworks are employed to invoke engagement and reflection. Compositions, improvisations and performances embrace interaction, environmental presence, autonomy, communication and transformation.

Bio

Accomplished and collaborative interdisciplinary artist and strategic educational leader with extensive teaching, administrative and creative direction experience. Expertise in studio practice, experimental composition, improvisation, interactivity and intermedia art. A dedicated practitioner and educator with acute focus on student success, efficient and effective project actualization, and fostering high morale. A passionate advocate for diverse and exploratory approaches to sound art, and developing innovative technologies to enable new forms of creativity and expression.

Chris is currently Academic Department Chair at Expression College, the Creative Director of THE STUDI/O – a technical and creative design, production and consulting company, and previously the Founder and Director of creative arts training centers. Chris is completing his dissertation at The University of Edinburgh on the applications of human-computer interaction and machine learning in interactive music environments. For years, Chris worked closely with one of Billboard Magazine’s Top Ten Producers of all time, 57 #1 Hits, multi-Grammy, Emmy & multi-Platinum Producer & Artist Narada Michael Walden at Tarpan Studios. Chris is a sought after Producer, Engineer, Composer, Arranger and Performer, and has done work for artists including Sting, Elton John, James Taylor, Billy Joel, Smokey Robinson, Scientist, PSY, Playboy, GQ, Vogue, Salvatore Ferragamo, and Katy Perry.
